Different Types of Cancer

Breast Cancer

Cancer that starts in the cells of the breast, commonly affecting women but can also occur in men.
Lung Cancer

Often caused by smoking, it originates in the lungs and can spread rapidly to other parts of the body.
Skin Cancer

Primarily caused by exposure to ultraviolet (UV) rays from the sun or tanning beds, including melanoma and non-melanoma types.
Leukemia

A cancer of the blood and bone marrow, characterized by the rapid production of abnormal white blood cells.
